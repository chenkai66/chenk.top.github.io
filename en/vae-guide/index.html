A Variational Autoencoder (VAE) is a generative model that learns a latent-variable distribution so it can both reconstruct inputs and sample new data. The key engineering trick is the reparameterization trick, which makes stochastic sampling differentiable.

1. Autoencoders in one paragraph

An autoencoder has an encoder (q_ heta(zx)) and a decoder (p_(xz)). It learns to compress (x) into (z), then reconstruct (x).
