Vocabulary Daily

7月 16, 2022

Vocabulary

  1. tollbooth: is an enclosure placed along a toll road that is used for the purpose of collecting a toll from passing traffic
  2. mile: 1 mile is equal to 1.609344 kilometres
  3. eject: banish, discharge, dismiss, expel
  4. gravity: earnest, sobriety, solemnness, staidness
  5. ornament: adornment, embellishment, decoration
  6. lugubrious: dejected, melancholy, gloomy, pessimistic. Opposite to optimistic, sanguine, rosy, buoyant
  7. postulate: assume, conjecture, hypothesize, premise, presume
  8. provocative: inflammatory, inciting, instigating, stimulating, arousing

Phrase

  1. a pit stop: a stop at a pit for servicing and refuelling, especially during a race
  2. exert one's authority
  3. break a taboo

Sentence

  1. Let's hit the road! My first road trip!
  2. He exert all his authority to make them accept the plan.
